How Good Was Tiger Woods?

A Look at the Greatest Golfer of All Time

3 Things That Make Him the Greatest

  1. Major Championships (15 Total)
    • Masters Tournament (5 wins: 1997, 2001, 2002, 2005, 2019)
    • PGA Championship (4 wins: 1999, 2000, 2006, 2007)
    • U.S. Open (3 wins: 2000, 2002, 2008)
    • The Open Championship (3 wins: 2000, 2005, 2006)
  2. PGA Tour Records
    • 82 all-time PGA Tour victories
    • 11-time PGA Tour Player of the Year
    • Most consecutive weeks at World No. 1 (281 weeks)
  3. Historic Milestones
    • Youngest player to achieve the career Grand Slam (age 24)
    • Winner of the "Tiger Slam" (held all four major titles simultaneously in 2000-2001)
    • Awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom (2019)
    • Inducted into the World Golf Hall of Fame (2022)
